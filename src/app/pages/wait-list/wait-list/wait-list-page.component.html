Introduction

Welcome to Collaborare! We prioritize your privacy and are dedicated to protecting your personal information. This policy details our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection as you engage with the Collaborare platform

Information We Collect
  • Patient contact group information: We collect your email address, patient organization and country when you register an account or interact with our services.
  • Patient health group information: We collect health information specific to your patient group in an aggregated manner (consolidated list at group level), not at an individual patient level. In any case, this information is treated with the highest confidentiality.
How We Use Your Information
  • To manage and maintain your user account.
  • To communicate with you about platform updates and to offer support.
  • To facilitate your participation in the platform.
  • To comply with legal obligations and ensure regulatory compliance.
Data Management and Storage
  • Only your email, patient organization, country and the list of items associated with your organiation are stored by Collaborare.
