Who is behind this initiative?

This initiative is backed by prominent entities within the rare diseases sector:

  • EURORDIS - Rare Diseases Europe: A coalition of patient organizations advocating for better lives for those affected by rare diseases.
  • European Medicines Agency (EMA): The EU body tasked with the scientific assessment, oversight, and safety supervision of drug development.
  • Foundation 29: A non-profit organization dedicated to leveraging data science and technology to tackle issues related to rare diseases.
